### 1. Difference between deleting and imputing missing data?
Deleting removes rows permanently; imputing replaces missing values using mean, median, or logic.

### 2. Risks of removing duplicates incorrectly?
You may lose valid records and distort analysis.

### 3. How do TRIM and CLEAN help?
TRIM removes extra spaces; CLEAN removes non-printable characters.

### 4. How do you validate data types?
Using formatting, filters, and Excel validation tools.

### 5. Why keep raw and cleaned data separate?
To preserve original data and ensure reproducibility.
